LED Game

Notice: This project isn't finished and may contain some bugs.

How to Play

Click on a yellow LED to gain a point. Once you did, you'll have a short amount of time to click on the next one. The game ends, if you miss a yellow diode, let to much time pass or, if you click on a red LED instead.
